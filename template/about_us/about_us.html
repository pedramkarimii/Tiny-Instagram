Community Guidelines

At Tiny Instagram, we believe in fostering a positive and inclusive community where everyone feels valued and respected. To maintain this environment, we have established the following community guidelines:

  • Respect and Kindness: Treat others with kindness, empathy, and respect. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, and discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • Authenticity: Be yourself and share genuine content. Do not impersonate others or engage in deceptive practices.
  • Safety and Privacy: Protect your privacy and the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent, and report any suspicious or harmful behavior.
  • Intellectual Property: Respect the intellectual property rights of others. Only share content that you have the right to share, and give proper credit when necessary.
  • Community Contribution: Contribute positively to the community by sharing constructive feedback, supporting fellow members, and participating in discussions.
  • Compliance with Laws: Follow all applicable laws and regulations when using Tiny Instagram. Illegal activities, such as promoting violence or engaging in illegal behavior, are strictly prohibited.

By adhering to these guidelines, we can create a vibrant and welcoming community where everyone can freely express themselves and connect with others through their shared experiences.
